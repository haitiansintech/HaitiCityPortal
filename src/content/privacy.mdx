# Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and only collect information needed to deliver municipal services.

## What we collect

- Details you provide on forms, such as contact information
- Usage analytics to improve system reliability
- Uploaded files for issues or title requests

## How we use data

We use data to communicate with you about requests, coordinate service responses, and publish aggregated statistics. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

## Your choices

You can request deletion of personal data or opt out of communications by emailing **privacy@haiticity.org**.
