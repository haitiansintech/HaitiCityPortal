# About Haiti City Portal

Haiti City Portal is a civic platform that connects residents with local services, data, and opportunities for collaboration. We work alongside municipal teams and community organizations to streamline public service delivery and make local information easier to access.

## Our mission

- Modernize how residents report issues and receive updates
- Provide transparent access to open datasets and maps
- Coordinate community outreach, events, and emergency information
